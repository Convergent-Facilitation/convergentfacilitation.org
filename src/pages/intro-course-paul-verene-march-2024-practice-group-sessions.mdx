# *Practice Group Sessions* for Radical Collaboration ‚Äî An Introduction to Convergent Facilitation 

We are delighted to have a list of practice sessions covering most days of the week and all timezones. This is an integral part of the course. Thanks to all of the facilitators who stepped in to facilitate these sessions. 

We want to highlight again how important practice is to integrate the method and start making a real difference as to how decisions are made in the world. We would therefore deeply appreciate for everyone to stretch if needs be and signs up to one of the sessions. There is an option to self-organise if none of them matches your availability (see below).  

## How to register for a practice group session?

<p style={{ fontSize: '22px' }}>
If you haven't done it already, please [register for the course](/intro-course-paul-verene-march-2024#where-and-when-is-it-happening). As soon as your participation is confirmed, **we will send you a link to a form via email** (search your inbox for "Practice Group Registration") where you can register for a practice group. 

</p>

You've already registered but didn't receive a link yet? Please send us an email to [mar24-intro-course@convergentfacilitation.org](mailto:mar24-intro-course@convergentfacilitation.org).

Please look at the list of sessions below and check which one fits your availability. Times and dates should automatically be displayed in your local time zone. If not, please take a look at [this spreadsheet](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/19qvjFelEOdZs-MSeEXlh-T6-tFJ28ZNEWrlaYc_Is7c/edit#gid=519258374) to find links to a time and date converter for each session. To register for a particular session, please note the name of the support person and coloured dot associated with it and fill in the registration form. 

üòïüïú **None of the offered time slots below is working for you? It will be possible to ‚≠ïself-organise with other participants to set up your own group. You'll see this option in the form. ‚è© Also, if you can make most, but not all sessions for a group, it is possible to miss a session.**
